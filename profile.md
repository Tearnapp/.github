# Tearn — Learn Anything, Anywhere

<img src="https://tearnapp.com/logo.png" alt="Tearn" width="200"/>

## The Mobile Marketplace Connecting Students with Trusted Trainers

Tearn makes it easy to discover and book skill-based lessons with verified trainers in your area. Whether you're looking to learn a sport, musical instrument, academic subject, or professional skill — Tearn connects you with the right trainer, handles booking and payments, and helps you grow your skills for real.

### 🔍 Discover
Browse hundreds of trainers by category, location, rating, and price. Read reviews from real students and find the perfect match for your learning goals.

### 📅 Book Instantly
View trainer availability in real-time and book sessions with a tap. No back-and-forth texting — everything happens in the app.

### 💳 Pay Seamlessly
Secure in-app payments. No cash, no hassle. Pay for single sessions or packages and track all your spending in one place.

### 🌟 For Trainers
- Create your trainer profile and list your skills
- Set your own schedule and rates
- Get discovered by students in your area
- Manage bookings, payments, and student messages all in one dashboard
